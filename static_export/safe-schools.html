Field Audit Diagnosis Findings

Primary water infrastructure is broken. 920 students share a single leaking rain barrel with zero chemical water-treatment supplies. Pit latrines are over capacity and pose serious sanitary hazards. Sinks are non-functional due to line damage. Immediate emergency piping retrofit and sink installation is required.
