W.A.S.H. EMPOWERMENT

Empowering Communities through Clean Water & Sanitation

Umoja Foundation acts as a vital bridge to restore hope, clean sanitation facilities, and sustainable rainwater infrastructure to East African schools.

Sponsor Water Access Explore Our Projects

Our Strategic Approach

Empowering remote primary schools and marginalized informal communities with reliable, climate-resilient water infrastructure.

H2O

Rainwater Collection Systems

Installing modern gutters, leaf-shedding screen systems, and robust multi-thousand liter water capture reservoirs in remote schools.

W

Active Hygiene Education

Engaging children directly with structured handwashing routines, sanitization education, and distributing custom soap and wash basins.

S

Independent W.A.S.H. Audits

Conducting rigorous, transparent inspections of school water facilities to ensure hygiene systems remain clean, functional, and safe.
