OUR HISTORY & MISSION

The Hearts Behind the Water

Founded in 2025, Umoja Foundation was born out of a critical mission: to eradicate preventable illnesses in East African primary schools by delivering reliable sanitization facilities and water capture systems.

Why Clean School Water Matters

When schools lack reliable drinking water and private, clean restrooms, children suffer. Health declines, resulting in high rates of gastrointestinal diseases. Girls are disproportionately affected, often missing days of school or dropping out completely once they reach puberty.

By investing in physical pipelines, secure rain reservoirs, and active handwashing education, Umoja Foundation builds spaces where safety, health, and classroom education can flourish together.

Our Core Principles

  • 01

    Long-Term Engineering

    We build steel-reinforced infrastructure that handles adverse climate shifts, rather than temporary plastic fixes.

  • 02

    Community Governance

    Every project forms a local maintenance board to ensure direct repairs, hygiene monitoring, and physical sustainability.

  • 03

    Transparent Stewardship

    Every dollar donated is mapped directly to a physical water tank, pipeline network, or chlorine replenishment block.
